A Fleet Management Dashboard is a comprehensive tool designed to provide insights into the performance, utilization, and maintenance of a fleet of vehicles.
It consolidates data into an interactive interface, enabling decision-makers to monitor operations, identify inefficiencies, and ensure optimal fleet performance.

Benefits
Operational Efficiency: Reduces downtime and improves vehicle utilization.
Cost Savings: Identifies cost-saving opportunities in fuel and maintenance.
Enhanced Safety: Promotes safer driving habits through monitoring.
Data-Driven Decisions: Empowers managers with actionable insights.
